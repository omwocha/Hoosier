Hoosier Camp Meeting App (Demo)

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Overview

The Hoosier Camp Meeting App respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may collect:

  • Account information: name, email address, authentication provider (email/password or Google)
  • Optional profile information: church affiliation or demographic details (if provided)
  • User-submitted content: prayer requests, feedback, or messages you choose to submit
  • Technical data: limited browser and session data necessary for app functionality

We do not collect or store payment or credit card information.

3. Authentication and Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in using Google:

  • Authentication is handled by Google’s secure systems
  • We receive basic profile information (such as name and email address)
  • Use of Google Sign-In is subject to Google’s privacy policies

Google logos and branding are used solely to indicate the sign-in option.

4. How We Use Information

Information collected through the App is used to:

  • Provide access to app features
  • Enable personalized functionality
  • Communicate important event-related updates
  • Improve the App experience

Data is not sold, rented, or shared for commercial purposes.

5. Donations and AdventistGiving

When you choose to donate, you are redirected to AdventistGiving, an external platform.

AdventistGiving:

  • Handles all donation processing
  • Does not share your payment details with this App
  • Operates under its own Privacy Policy

We encourage users to review AdventistGiving’s Privacy Policy for full details regarding donations.

6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The App uses limited cookies and local storage for:

  • Authentication sessions
  • Security
  • Basic functionality

These may include temporary cookies required for login. Disabling cookies may limit functionality.

A cookie consent notice is provided for transparency and compliance.

7. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored using Google Firebase services and protected with industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit.

Access to administrative data is restricted by role-based permissions.

8. Data Retention

Demo data may be periodically reset. Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for the App’s intended purpose or demonstration lifecycle.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location (including California, Texas, Indiana, Kentucky, or the European Union), you may have rights to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

Requests can be made through the App administrators.

10. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended to independently collect personal information from children without parental involvement.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in functionality or legal requirements. Updates will be posted within the App.

12. Contact

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy may be directed to the Camp Meeting administrative team.
